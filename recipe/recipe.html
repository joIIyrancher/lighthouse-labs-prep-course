Kraft's Super Easy Peanut Butter Cookies

When we need cookies in a snap, this is our best go-to recipe - easy, fast and super delicious!

  • Prep Time: 10 min.
  • Total Time: 30 min.
  • Servings: 24 servings, 1 cookie (16 g) each

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Kraft Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions

Heat oven to 325Â°F.
Mix all ingredients with large spoon until well blended.
Roll into 24 balls; place, 4 inches apart, on baking sheets. Flatten with fork.
Bake 20 min. or until lightly browned. (Do not overbake.) Cool 5 min. on baking sheets; transfer to wire racks. Cool completely.
